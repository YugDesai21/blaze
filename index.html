About Blaze

The National Level Sports Festival of ASMSOC, NMIMS. The Sports Festival consists of Football, Basketball, and Cricket as their main sporting events. It unites talent, teamwork and the spirit of competition of more than 5000+ sports enthusiasts from 50+ colleges.

Blaze will step into its 8th edition this year and we are ecstatic to make it an unforgettable experience with your engagement and cooperation while giving your brand unmatched reach.

We have welcomed colleges from Pune, Haryana, Bangalore, Kolkata, Delhi, and the surrounding area of Mumbai. We are proud to have hosted an international team from Babson College in Boston, USA.
